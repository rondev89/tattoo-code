Welcome to Killer Ink Studio

At Killer Ink Studio, we believe tattoos are more than just art—they’re personal stories etched into skin. Founded in 2016, our studio has become a trusted name in bold, clean, and custom tattooing.

Our artists bring a diverse range of styles—from traditional to neo-traditional, blackwork to realism—and are committed to making your vision come to life with precision and passion.

Located in the heart of the city, Killer Ink Studio is known for its welcoming vibe, strict hygiene standards, and a client-first approach. Whether it's your first tattoo or you're adding to your collection, we’ve got you covered.

Come visit us. Let’s create something unforgettable together.

— The Killer Ink Crew
